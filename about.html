About PAWJ

Our mission, history, and the people behind the association.

Mission & Cause

PAWJ (Parent Association for Welfare & Justice) exists to empower parents and students by championing transparent, fair, and accountable practices in school education. Founded to address systemic gaps in rights awareness and to challenge exploitative fee policies, PAWJ believes every family deserves access to inclusive, affordable learning. We work together to ensure schools comply with laws and standards, support parents’ voices in decision-making, and protect children’s interests through lawful advocacy, community education, and collective action. Our mission is to build a strong, informed parent community that stands united for justice, quality education, and the well-being of every child.
